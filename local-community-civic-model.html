Civic progression model

Tonal weights across four stages of community engagement

Weight scale: 1 (minimal) → 5 (dominant)

⚠ Sensitivity mode active: Grounded (5) + Practical (5) prioritised, Creative (1) + Optimistic (1) suppressed

Responsive design

This model is designed for optimal clarity at 1200-1440px width and scales fluidly down to 960px before stacking vertically on smaller screens.

Desktop (≥1200px): 4-column comparison view · Tablet (768-1199px): Reduced gutters, maintained comparison · Mobile (≤767px): Vertical stack with full-width blocks

Grounded baseline throughout

Human and grounded maintains weight 5 (or 4) across all stages, ensuring warmth, accessibility, and civic calm remain constant.

Collective agency peaks at Partner

Collectively driven grows from weight 2 → 4 → 5, showing how shared ownership emerges through genuine participation and co-creation.

Earned optimism at Impact

Boldly optimistic stays minimal (1-2) until Impact where it reaches weight 4. Hope emerges from sustained collective action, not early promises.

Practical clarity for governance

Purposefully practical remains strong (3-5) throughout, peaking at Partner. This reflects the need for clear processes and accountable collaboration.
